Guatemala City, Chamber of Commerce Plan

Site Name

Guatemala City - Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates

Site Purpose

The Guatemala City Chamber of Commerce, works to facilitate networking and collaboration among local businesses promoting economic growth, and also provides with a centralized platform for residents to access information about local events and services

Scenarios

1. What type of events will the chamber be holding this month?

2. How can I join and get an insight of the benefits of the Chamber?

3. Where can I contact the chamber's board of directors?
